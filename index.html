    TAIWO MERCY

    Damilola speaks

    Taiwo attended the same school (OAU Ife) and fellowship (RCF) with me. We often met at church and reading room.

    I began to notice virtues that I treasure so much in her. I realized that she is a very simple, beautiful, friendly and caring lady. These attributes attracted me to her.

    I made this decision after a few years of friendship when the relationship kept getting better and I was pleased with every bit of her life style. I realized that we have common goals and she is the best woman to help me achieve my personal goals.

    DAMILOLA EMMANUEL

    Taiwo speaks

    I noticed Damilola during an event decoration training organized by his department (publicity unit) at the fellowship (rcf) I attended on campus (oau ife). He was the leader of the unit and he was very good at conceptualization which is the most important aspect of any event decoration. So I admired him so much for that.

    The first thing that attracted me to him was his intelligence. Asides this, I also noticed some virtues in him. Such virtues include; simplicity, spirituality, patience, kindness, and generosity.

    The reason for my choice is because I consider him the best man for me. He is the will of God for me.
